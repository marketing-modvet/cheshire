📖 What changed

The previous Veta worked, but she sounded like a chatbot — opening every reply with "Great question!", decorating sentences with 🐾 emoji, dumping markdown bullet lists, and pushing booking on every turn. Real Modern Vet staff don't talk like that. We rebuilt her.

The approach

Instead of guessing at "what human Modern Vet staff sound like", we mined 422,545 actual outbound WhatsApp messages from the call centre, plus 22,721 phone-call transcripts and 18 official SOPs, and built a tone & content brief grounded in that real evidence. Every entry in the new KB was rewritten by Opus 4.7 against that brief, with brand rules (Modern Vet only, with carved-out exceptions for charity strays → Vienna Vet and exotic surgery/CT scans → Blue Oasis Vet) enforced on every entry.

The persona shift

Veta is now presented as a real human team member, not a chatbot. When she needs to verify something, she stalls naturally — "hold on, let me check" — like any front-desk staff would, then comes back with the answer. There is no "team" or "manager" she transfers to. She IS the team.

The persona test that mattered most:
User: "Can I speak to a human please?"
Old Veta: "Sure — let me get one of the team to jump in. One moment."
New Veta: "You are — it's me, Veta. What do you need a hand with?"

📝 How to give feedback

Veta's only as good as the conversations she has. Help us tune her by chatting with her and flagging anything off — wrong info, weird tone, missing answers, or just things that feel like a chatbot.

⭐ Primary: give feedback right in the chat

  1. Open the chat. Click this link — no login needed.
  2. Talk to her like a real client. Ask about prices, branches, doctors, your pet's situation. Test edge cases — random neighbourhoods, tricky bookings, complaints, emergencies.
  3. 👍 / 👎 on any reply. Hover over Veta's message and the thumbs appear. If you thumbs-down, you can add a quick note about what was wrong. This is the most useful kind of feedback — it pinpoints the exact bad answer.
  4. ⭐ Star rating at the end when ElevenLabs prompts you for an overall rating.

📧 Secondary: email Andrew for complex issues

If something doesn't fit a thumbs-down (policy question, multi-turn issue, screenshot you want to share), email marketing@modernvet.com with the question, her answer, and what was wrong.

📅 Friday review

Every Friday morning, Andrew sits with the system to go through the week's in-chat feedback (👍/👎/⭐ + comments) and any emails. Issues get triaged, KB gets updated, Veta is smarter the following week. Closing the loop is part of the routine — your feedback won't get lost.

If you find a question Veta says she "doesn't have to hand", that's a known gap — see the gaps report for the full list. Aida and Operations are working through those.
