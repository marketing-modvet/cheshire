05 · The honest part

Where we are constrained.

Naming the friction is part of owning the result. Three structural challenges are shaping the H2 2026 plan.

Structural · Brand spread

Seven brands, one team.

MV · BOV · VV · TVRC · BSPR · Floof · VA — each needs its own brand voice, its own social presence, its own printables, its own campaigns. The same headcount that produces one brand's output is now distributed across seven.

Mitigation in plan: shared production engine + brand-specific briefs · sequenced campaigns instead of parallel ones · template & design-system reuse.
Perception · Output dilution

Output looks lower because it is thinned across seven brands.

500+ posts and 8 000+ photos sound large in aggregate, but split across 40 social accounts the per-channel cadence reads as modest. The work is there — the surface area hides it.

Mitigation in plan: consolidated reporting (this dashboard) · per-brand dashboards · output-per-FTE benchmark vs market.
External · SEO & AI Overviews

Google's AI Overviews are eroding blog traffic.

The new AI section in Google search is taking a measurable share of clicks that previously landed on our Health Library and similar long-tail content. This is industry-wide, not Modern Vet specific.

Mitigation in plan: EEAT depth & doctor-attributed content (not commodity) · YouTube as second front (not algorithm-controlled the same way) · brand-search optimisation & partnerships.
