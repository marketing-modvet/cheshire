v5.2 — Same-day Conversational Patch

After v5.1 deployed, Andrew tested it and flagged a reply that felt too long, info-dumped both vaccine packages, and asked for the pet's name out of the blue. v5.2 fixes those patterns with 5 strict conversational rules.

  • Max ~50-60 words per reply — WhatsApp rhythm, not email rhythm.
  • Never dump both vaccine packages — ask "first round or up to date?" first, then send only the relevant one.
  • Pet's name only for active booking / records / pre-op — never asked out of the blue for pricing or location queries.
  • One follow-up question at a time — don't pile up.
  • Banned phrases extended — "Happy to help / Happy to assist / Glad to help" all out.

19 KB entries rewritten in this patch ($0.20 Opus spend). System prompt + KB redeployed to both surfaces.
