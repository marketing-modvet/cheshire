Sample Before/After

Real entries from the KB, showing the rewrite work.

Q: "I live in DIP. Where's the closest vet?"

v2 (fabricated, pushy): Your closest is JVC, about 7.8 km away — Platinum Residences. Consult AED 249 plus VAT. Want me to book you in?
v5.5 (real data, concise, no push): From DIP, your closest is MV JVC — around 15-20 min off-peak (12 km), open 7am–10pm. Consultation's AED 249 plus VAT.

Q: "Blue Oasis is closer to me. Can you match their price?"

v2 (mischaracterises BOV + aggressive close): Blue Oasis specialises in exotics. For your dog I'd come to us — JVC is nearby. Want me to book you in?
v5.5 (polite, firm decline, no Mobile-Clinic deflection): We don't do price matching, sorry. Our consultations are AED 249 at branches and AED 299 at the Hospital plus VAT. If you'd like to come to us we'd be glad to have you.

Q: "Do you do vaccinations?"

v2 (dumps both packages, 130+ words): Yes, full vaccinations for dogs and cats. If your pet's up to date, the Annual Vaccine Package is AED 349 at branches or AED 535 at Hospital — consult, core vaccine, rabies, deworming and the municipality tag. If they're new or skipped last year, the New Pet Package covers two consults and two sets of vaccines plus rabies, deworming and the tag — AED 549 at branches, AED 699 at Hospital. When were they last vaccinated?
v5.5 (asks first, sends the right one): Yes, we do full vaccinations for dogs and cats. Quick question — is this their first round, or are they up to date from last year? I'll send you the right package.

Q: "Booking confirmation — JVC, 2pm tomorrow"

v2 (decorative emoji, "Perfect." opener): Perfect. Booked at JVC for tomorrow 2pm. Anything else? 👍
v5.5 (natural, warm, no emoji): Booked at JVC for tomorrow 2pm. See you then — let me know if anything changes.

Patch History (May 18)

Three iterations shipped same day based on real-feedback testing.

  • v5.1 — Major rebuild: 1,313 → 1,900 entries. 374 entries rewritten for tone. Real Google Routes distances pulled for 74 Dubai areas. System prompt extended with banned-phrase rules + BOV mischaracterisation guard.
  • v5.2 — Conversational patch: WhatsApp rhythm (max 50-60 words per reply), never dump both vaccine packages without asking client first, pet's name only requested when booking is active. 19 entries rewritten.
  • v5.3 — Concise call-centre tone: area-routing answers compressed to ~30 words (closest branch + price only), Mobile Clinic stops being auto-tacked-on to every reply, polite firm decline on price-matching requests. 395 entries rewritten.
  • v5.4 — Dash sweep: all 1,293 em-dash and non-range en-dash entries rewritten by Opus to remove the biggest WhatsApp AI tell. Filler words (honestly, essentially, basically) stripped. Run-on sentences smoothed. System prompt updated with explicit dash ban + filler ban. Zero em dashes remaining in KB.
  • v5.5 — Quality pass: Opus scored all 1,900 entries for WhatsApp naturalness. 159 entries rewritten (formulaic doctor bios, weak openers, robotic phrasing, stiff connectors, awkward comma splices). Score distribution: 1,053 perfect (5), 688 good (4), 159 fixed (≤3). Total Opus spend $37.82.

What's Next
