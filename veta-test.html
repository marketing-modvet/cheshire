Veta — Modern Vet Assistant

Internal test page for the team to chat with Veta and tell us where she shines and where she trips up.

How to test 🐾

Click the chat bubble in the bottom-right corner to start. Type as if you were a real client messaging Modern Vet on WhatsApp — ask about pricing, services, doctors, branches, packages, emergencies, anything you'd realistically ask.

  • Have a proper back-and-forth, not just one question. We want to see how she handles a real conversation flow.
  • Try to stump her — edge cases, unusual species, weird scheduling, complaints, multi-pet questions.
  • Mix easy and hard — softball questions confirm she gets the basics right; hard ones show us the gaps.
Heads up: Veta presents as a real Modern Vet team member, not as AI. She also can't pull live ezyVet data (appointments, records, stock) — that's deliberate. If she stalls on those, that's correct behaviour.

How to give feedback 📝

ElevenLabs recently removed the per-message thumbs up / down buttons from the chat widget, so we're collecting feedback two other ways:

  • End-of-chat star rating — when you close the chat window, you'll be asked for a 1-5 star rating and an optional comment. Please use it every time — that's our main quality signal.
  • Slack — #veta-feedback — for anything specific (a reply was wrong, off-tone, invented a price, missed an obvious answer), drop a screenshot or quote in Slack with a quick note. That's how we catch the stuff that needs fixing in the knowledge base.
Behind the scenes: every conversation transcript is logged automatically. Andrew + Cheshire run them through a review pipeline weekly to flag issues and feed corrections back into Veta's knowledge base — your feedback speeds that up massively.

What we're testing

  • Accuracy — does she get prices, branches, hours, doctors right?
  • Tone — does she sound like a sharp human teammate, not a corporate bot?
  • Boundaries — does she stall naturally on things she shouldn't have (e.g. live system data)?
  • Brand — does she stay Modern Vet without leaking BOV / VV?
  • Booking flow — can she take a booking smoothly (pet name, service, time, branch, phone)?
