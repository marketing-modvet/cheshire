Veta — Modern Vet Assistant

Embedded test page so the team can chat with Veta and leave thumbs up / thumbs down feedback on each reply.

How to test 🐾

Click the chat bubble in the bottom-right corner to start. Type as if you were a real client asking about Modern Vet — pricing, services, doctors, branches, packages, anything.

  • Thumbs up / down appear on each of Veta's replies — please tap them as you go.
  • If a reply is wrong, off-tone, or invented, give it a thumbs down.
  • If a reply nails it, thumbs up so we know what's working.
  • End-of-chat star rating is also enabled — give an overall score before you close the window.
Heads up: Veta presents as a real Modern Vet team member, not as AI. She also can't pull live ezyVet data (appointments, records) — that's deliberate.

What we're testing

  • Accuracy — does she get prices, branches, hours, doctors right?
  • Tone — does she sound like a sharp human teammate, not a corporate bot?
  • Boundaries — does she stall naturally on things she shouldn't have (e.g. live system data)?
  • Brand — does she stay Modern Vet without leaking BOV / VV?
