About Vet Alliance

Vet Alliance Holding is the UAE's largest veterinary group, operating a network of clinics across Dubai under a single quality and clinical standard. We provide preventive, routine, emergency, surgical and referral-level care for companion animals, working dogs, and institutional patients.

Certifications & Quality

Every clinic in the network is fully licensed by the Dubai Municipality and audited against a unified clinical and infection-control standard. All clinics operate on the same medical records system, which means a patient's full history follows them between locations.

Our Network

A 24/7 multi-specialty hospital, a referral centre (TVRC), seven general-practice clinics across Dubai, and a fully-equipped mobile veterinary unit. A single coordinated team across the full clinical pathway.

Clinical Team

Over 70 veterinarians across the group, supported by registered veterinary nurses and trained support staff. The team spans more than 30 languages and covers internal medicine, surgery, orthopaedics, cardiology, neurology, ophthalmology, dermatology, oncology, and rehabilitation.

Operational Reliability

Shared diagnostics, shared records, shared protocols. Cases can move between a general-practice clinic and the hospital without losing continuity, and complex cases route to the referral centre under one coordinated team. Open every day of the year, including public holidays.

Government & Institutional Veterinary Services

Vet Alliance supports government, security, and institutional partners that operate working dogs and other service animals. We provide structured, accountable veterinary care backed by the full diagnostic and surgical capability of the group — from a routine vaccine round at a kennel through to overnight emergency surgery on a working dog.

Care For Working Dogs

Dedicated clinical handling for K9 units and service-dog programmes, including high-drive working breeds and patients in active deployment.

Preventive & Routine

Annual vaccinations, scheduled health checks, parasite control, dental scaling, microchipping, and kennel-side wellness assessments.

Emergency & Urgent Care

24/7 walk-in emergency triage at Modern Vet Hospital. Direct line for partner referrals with no booking delays.

Diagnostics, Surgery & Hospitalisation

In-house lab, X-ray, ultrasound, CT and MRI. Surgical theatres for soft-tissue, orthopaedic, and neurological work. Inpatient wards with overnight monitoring.

Medical Reporting & Case Documentation

Full written case reports, treatment summaries, imaging exports, and follow-up plans issued for every institutional patient. Records auditable on request.

Dedicated Coordination

A single named contact handles bookings, follow-ups, and escalations for the partner. Bypasses the standard call-centre queue with priority routing.

How partnerships are structured. Each institutional partner is set up with a master account covering all enrolled animals, agreed pricing, monthly consolidated billing, and a named coordinator. Reports, vaccination certificates, and case files are issued on the partner's letterhead requirements where needed.

Referral Hospital

TVRC — The Veterinary Referral Centre

UAE's First Referral-Only Specialty Hospital

TVRC is the UAE's first dedicated referral-only specialty hospital. It accepts patients on referral or second-opinion basis from veterinary partners and does not run first-opinion or vaccination services.

The team consists of board-certified specialists with international training and experience. TVRC is the escalation route for any case in the network that requires specialist input — complex neurosurgery, cardiology, ophthalmology, oncology and chemotherapy, advanced dermatology, and MRI/CT imaging.
