Combined effect: Modern Vet is now acquiring YouTube subscribers at 1/180th of the prior cost, with 29% less ad spend. The same marketing dirham now produces ~180× more channel growth than it did six months ago.

What Drove the Improvement

  • Targeting & tagging optimization — view-through rate climbed from 0.55% to 6.25% (×11). People who see the ads now actually choose to watch.
  • Higher-quality content production — subscriber conversion per view rose from 2.3% to 21.4% (×9). Viewers stick, find value, and follow.
  • Blog-quality scripts feeding video — topics like "Pet Nutrition Myths," "Pet Emergencies," "Pet Surgery Isn't Scary" are evergreen, search-friendly, and resonate with pet owners' actual questions.
  • Shorts & emotional content mix — BTS, rescue stories, and adoption clips (Dangle test on kittens, From paralyzed to walking) add audience depth at very low cost.
