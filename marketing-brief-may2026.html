Traffic & Online Bookings
• Website traffic up 49% this month — Modern Vet reached 55,000 visits (from 37K), Vienna Vet averaged 20,000, Blue Oasis 4,000. Combined group web traffic: 79,000+ visits.
• 84 confirmed online bookings captured directly from the website. Conversion tracking now live and verified across Modern Vet, Vienna Vet, and Blue Oasis — real transaction data flowing into Google Ads and GA4.

YouTube
• The channel grew by 107,000 subscribers in a single 28-day period — now at 117,000 total. Monthly views hit 926,000 (a 310% increase vs baseline). Top-performing video crossed 190,000 views. This puts the group channel in a strong position for organic authority and paid-media amplification.

SEO & AI Visibility
• 25% of tracked keywords now rank in Google's Top 10 — up 5 percentage points month-on-month. Average position improved to 21. The keyword "vets" jumped 29 positions in a single month.
• Modern Vet is now cited as a source in Google AI Overviews, Microsoft Copilot, and ChatGPT — a significant EEAT signal that reflects the depth of content and structured data work completed this year.
• Organic traffic from the UAE grew by 63% month-on-month per Ahrefs. Domain authority: 53, with 12,800 backlinks and 923 referring domains.

Social Media
• +5,000 new followers across all platforms this month. 40 social accounts actively managed across 7 brands.
