Comprehensive Veterinary Services in Dubai

Modern Vet has been Dubai's leading veterinary group since 1995, providing the full spectrum of pet healthcare across six conveniently located clinics and one 24-hour emergency hospital. Whether your pet needs routine vaccinations, a specialist consultation, complex surgery, or same-day emergency treatment, our multi-disciplinary team of veterinarians and veterinary nurses is equipped to help.

Our services cover every stage of your pet's life — from puppy and kitten wellness programs through to geriatric care, chronic disease management, and end-of-life support. We treat dogs, cats, and exotic animals, with breed-specific expertise and evidence-based protocols that align with international standards of care.

Beyond clinical services, Modern Vet offers convenient solutions for busy Dubai pet owners: pet taxi, food delivery, mobile clinic visits, and full international pet relocation assistance. Our team handles the paperwork so your pet travels safely and legally wherever life takes you.
